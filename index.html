Privacy Policy.

How R&B Fitness collects, uses, and protects your personal information under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Last updated: June 2026

Data controller

R&B Fitness is the data controller responsible for your personal data. We are a private personal training centre based at 9 Bedford Business Centre, Mile Road, Bedford MK42 9TW. You can contact us at randbfitnessptcentre@gmail.com or 07769 485074.

What information we collect

We collect the following personal data:

  • Enquiry form: your name, email address, phone number (if provided), and fitness goals
  • Chatbot: your name, contact details, and any information you share during the conversation
  • Members: health and fitness data, training records, body composition measurements, nutrition logs, and payment information necessary to deliver our coaching services

Lawful basis for processing

Under UK GDPR Article 6, we process your data on the following lawful bases:

  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a)): when you submit an enquiry form or engage with our chatbot, you consent to us contacting you about your consultation
  • Contract (Article 6(1)(b)): when you become a member, processing is necessary to deliver the coaching services you have signed up for
  • Legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f)): to improve our services and ensure the security of our systems

Where we process special category data (health and fitness information), we do so with your explicit consent under Article 9(2)(a).

How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiry and arrange your free consultation
  • To deliver personalised coaching, training programmes, and nutrition guidance
  • To send you relevant information about our services (only with your consent)
  • To communicate with you via WhatsApp, email, or phone about your membership

Third parties and data processors

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data may be processed by:

  • Google (Gmail): email communication (US-based, covered by Data Privacy Framework)
  • Cloudflare: website security and performance (US-based, covered by Data Privacy Framework)
  • Vercel: website hosting (US-based, covered by Data Privacy Framework)
  • Stripe: payment processing (US-based, covered by Data Privacy Framework)
  • Meta (WhatsApp): messaging (US-based, covered by Data Privacy Framework)

All processors are bound by appropriate safeguards under UK GDPR Chapter V for international transfers.

Data retention

  • Enquiries: if you do not become a client, we delete your data within 12 months
  • Members: we retain your data for the duration of your membership and for up to 24 months after it ends, unless required by law to retain it longer
  • Financial records: retained for 6 years as required by HMRC

Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you (Subject Access Request)
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erase your data (the "right to be forgotten"), where there is no compelling reason to continue processing
  • Restrict or object to processing in certain circumstances
  • Data portability: receive your data in a structured, commonly used format
  • Withdraw consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal

To exercise any of these rights, email randbfitnessptcentre@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies

This website uses only strictly necessary cookies (a single preference to remember that you have seen the cookie notice). We do not use any tracking, analytics, or advertising cookies. No personal data is collected through cookies.

Data security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure hosting, and access controls. In the unlikely event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify the ICO within 72 hours and inform you without undue delay.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top will always reflect the most recent version. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact and complaints

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at randbfitnessptcentre@gmail.com or call 07769 485074.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk or by calling 0303 123 1113.
